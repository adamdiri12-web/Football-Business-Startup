About Saha Sports
Built by players,
for players

Saha Sports started with three friends and one frustration — we loved the game but could never find a reliable run. Group chats went quiet, plans fell through, and half the time we'd show up to an empty field.

So we built what we wished existed. Now we book fields across the GTA, organize fair teams, and make it dead simple for anyone to just show up and play. Whether you're sharpening up for league season or just need to get a kick in after work — there's always a game waiting for you.
